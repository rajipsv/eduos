Pillar 1 — Education CRM

Product: EduOS · Layer: 1 · Sidebar: Education CRM

Purpose

Education CRM is your sales-to-operations pipeline. It captures inquiries from every channel, tracks prospects through demo and admission stages, and converts enrolled leads into Academy students.

EduOS is ops-first — CRM handles enrollment logistics, not course content.

How to use — step by step

A. Set up your public website

  1. Go to Education CRM → Public Website → Page settings.
  2. Enter center name, tagline, phone, email, and courses list.
  3. Save and check Live preview.
  4. Share the public URL (marketplace / guest inquiry flows also feed CRM).

B. Add a lead manually

  1. Lead pipeline → + New Lead.
  2. Enter name, phone, grade, course interest, and source.
  3. Save — lead appears in Lead column.

C. Run a lead through the pipeline

  1. Open lead Details from a pipeline card.
  2. Set Demo date and Demo time when scheduling a trial class.
  3. Set Follow-up date for callbacks.
  4. Log activities (calls, notes) in the activity field.
  5. Click Advance → on the card to move one stage forward, or change stage in Details.
  6. Send WhatsApp follow-up from the lead card or detail modal.

D. Enroll a lead as a student

  1. Move lead to Batch Allocation stage.
  2. Click Enroll on the pipeline card (or in lead Details).
  3. Pick a batch and confirm parent/student details.
  4. Lead moves to Student (converted) and appears in Academy → Students.

Verify

  • [ ] Public website preview shows correct center info
  • [ ] Test inquiry creates a lead in CRM (source: Website)
  • [ ] Lead advances through stages; activity log records notes
  • [ ] Enroll creates a student in the chosen batch
  • [ ] Conversion stats update on CRM dashboard
