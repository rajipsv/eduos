# Pillar 8 — Business Intelligence

**Product:** EduOS · **Layer:** 8 · **Sidebar:** Business Intelligence

---

## Purpose

Business Intelligence tells the academy owner **what to do next** — not just what happened. It combines KPIs, trends, predictions, and priority decision cards so you act on attendance drops, full batches, and at-risk students before they become crises.

**Reports** (System → Reports) show historical tables; **BI** shows actionable priorities.

---

## Who uses it

| Role | Access |
|------|--------|
| Center admin | Full BI dashboard |
| Platform owner | Via support view into a center |
| Teachers / parents | No BI access |

---

## Modules & functionalities

**Path:** Business Intelligence

| Tab | Function |
|-----|----------|
| Decisions | Today's priority cards — high/medium/info with **Resolve** links to relevant module |
| KPIs | Headline metrics + recommended actions table |
| Trends | 4-week attendance chart, weekly sessions completed chart |
| Predictions | 30-day forecast cards + dropout risk register |
| Lead Analytics | Leads by source, conversion funnel |
| Batch Analytics | Per-batch enrollment, utilization, curriculum % |

**Sample decision types:**

- Attendance dropped this week → go to Attendance
- Batch over capacity → open parallel batch
- Batches without tutor → assign teachers
- Classes tomorrow → review schedule
- Leads in pipeline → follow up CRM
- AI insight alerts → review AI Assistants

**KPI metrics:** Overall attendance, avg test score, lead conversion, curriculum progress, capacity used, at-risk students, active leads, messages sent.

---

## How to use — step by step

### A. Start each day with Decisions

1. Open **Business Intelligence → Decisions**.
2. Read cards sorted by priority (high first).
3. Click **Resolve** on each — jumps to Attendance, Batches, Teachers, Schedule, CRM, or AI.
4. Take action in that module, then return to BI.

### B. Review KPI health

1. **KPIs tab** — scan stat cards.
2. Read **Metrics → Decisions** table for status badges (OK / Monitor / Action).
3. If attendance &lt; 80%, plan parent outreach via Communication Hub.
4. If capacity ≥ 90%, create a new batch in Academy.

### C. Monitor trends

1. **Trends tab** — compare 4-week attendance line chart.
2. Review weekly sessions marked completed — curriculum pace indicator.

### D. Act on predictions

1. **Predictions tab** — read 30-day forecast cards (confidence %).
2. **Dropout Risk Register** — students flagged high/medium risk.
3. Click **Intervene** → opens Student Success for intervention plans.

### E. Analyze leads and batches

1. **Lead Analytics** — which sources convert best; funnel drop-off stages.
2. **Batch Analytics** — utilization badges (green/orange/red); curriculum progress per batch.
3. Use insights to adjust marketing (CRM) and staffing (Teachers).

---

## Decisions vs Reports

| Business Intelligence | Reports (System) |
|----------------------|------------------|
| What should I do today? | How did we perform? |
| Priority cards with actions | Student & teacher performance tables |
| Predictions and risk register | Export-style summaries |
| Live KPI thresholds | Historical test/attendance averages |
