# Pillar 9 — Extensions & Partners

**Product:** EduOS · **Layer:** 9 · **Sidebar:** Extensions → Extensions & Partners

---

## Purpose

Extensions connect EduOS to **tools you already use** for teaching and business ops — Zoom, Google Calendar, WhatsApp, payroll partners, GST services, and message templates. EduOS remains the **operations hub**; lesson content stays on external platforms.

This is **not** a course content marketplace.

---

## Who uses it

| Role | Access |
|------|--------|
| Center admin | Browse, connect, disconnect extensions |
| Other roles | No marketplace access |

## Modules & functionalities

**Path:** Extensions & Partners

**Stats:** Integrations count, ops templates, partner services, active connections.

| Tab | Function |
|-----|----------|
| Browse | Search/filter integrations, templates, partner services — install or view details |
| Connected | Extensions already active in your academy |
| Connections | SDK-style tools (Zoom, Calendar, Twilio, etc.) — Connect / Disconnect toggles |

**Extension types:**

| Type | Examples |
|------|----------|
| Integration | Zoom Class Connector, Google Calendar sync |
| Ops template | Fee reminder packs, announcement templates |
| Partner service | Payroll, GST filing, fee reconciliation partners |

**Note:** OpenAI and WhatsApp API credentials are configured in **Settings**, not here — but Connections tab links you to that reminder.

## How to use — step by step

### A. Browse extensions

1. Open **Extensions & Partners → Browse**.
2. Search (e.g. "payroll", "GST", "Zoom") or filter by type.
3. Read description, author, price, and install count.
4. Click **Details** for full information.

### B. Connect an integration

1. On a card, click **Connect** (or **Install**).
2. Item moves to **Connected** tab with green badge.
3. Follow install hint on card (e.g. configure meeting platform on batches).

### C. Manage connected items

1. **Connected tab** — list of active extensions.
2. Click **Disconnect** to remove if no longer needed.

### D. SDK connections

1. **Connections tab** — see Zoom, Google Calendar, Twilio, etc.
2. Click **Connect** per tool — toggles installed state.
3. Use connected Zoom/Meet links when generating batch schedules (Academy → Batches).

### E. Partner services

1. Browse **Partner services** filter for payroll/GST help.
2. Partner handles salaries and tax — EduOS tracks **student fees** separately.
3. Contact partner via details on card if offered.

### F. Rate an extension

1. On Browse, click **Rate** (non-partner items).
2. Submit rating to help other academies choose.

## Recommended connections for new centers

| Priority | Extension / setting | Why |
|----------|---------------------|-----|
| 1 | WhatsApp (Settings) | Parent reminders and fee nudges |
| 2 | Zoom or Google Meet (batch platform) | Live class links in schedule |
| 3 | Google Calendar (Connections) | Sync sessions externally |
| 4 | Fee Reminder template pack | Pre-built comms templates |
| 5 | Payroll partner (optional) | Staff salaries outside EduOS |
