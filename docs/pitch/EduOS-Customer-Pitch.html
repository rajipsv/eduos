How centers use EduOS daily

Workflow 1 — From stranger to enrolled student

Website inquiry→ CRM lead→ Demo class→ Counseling→ Batch allocation→ Enrolled student

Capture leads from your public site, WhatsApp, walk-ins, and ads. Move them through a visual pipeline with follow-up reminders — then enroll in one click.

Workflow 2 — Running classes every week

Create batch→ Auto-schedule→ Meeting links→ Mark attendance→ Notify parents

Pick days and times, add topics, and EduOS generates the full term schedule with Google Meet, Zoom, or Teams links. Teachers mark attendance; parents get updates automatically.

Workflow 3 — Homework & parent visibility

Staff (admin & teachers)

Create assignments, issue certificates, post feedback, and review submissions. Interventions trigger when a student falls behind.

Parents & students

Parents see homework status and progress — read-only, no admin clutter. Students submit work; parents stay informed via portal and WhatsApp.

Workflow 4 — Owner decisions, not just reports

Business Intelligence goes beyond charts. EduOS surfaces today's decisions: which leads need follow-up, which students are at risk, which batches need attention — with one-click actions.
